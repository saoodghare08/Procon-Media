About Us

Welcome to Procon Media, a leading organizer of Business Networking Conferences, Exhibitions, and Events. We specialize in connecting industry leaders from Workplace, Administration and Facilities, Real Estate, Architecture, Infrastructure, and Sustainability sectors. Top leaders from various sectors come together for specially curated events where they engage in panel discussions, fireside chats, and debates on topics that are relevant, trending, and important. Our platform also enables vendors and businesses from diverse sectors to meet top corporates under one roof on one day, fostering valuable connections and business opportunities.

The Leadership Award Summit

A major highlight of our conferences is The Leadership Award Summit. This prestigious segment recognizes and awards senior industry professionals across various categories for their exceptional contributions to their respective workplaces. The awardees are nominated and recommended jointly by the management of Procon Group and senior industry leaders associated with us, ensuring a high standard of recognition.

Mission

At Procon Media, our Mission is to establish a platform where professionals from all industries have access to high-quality conferences, events, and tradeshows that help them stay abreast of the latest trends and innovations in their respective fields.

We strive to be the leading provider of professional conferences & events that deliver exceptional value to our attendees and help them build the connections and knowledge they need to succeed.

Vision

Our vision is to create a global community of professionals who are passionate about learning, collaborating, and driving innovation in their respective fields.
