About Us

At Procon Media, our vision is to create meaningful connections by bringing industry professionals together on a common platform through conferences, meetings, and exhibitions. Our current focus is on Real Estate, Architecture, Corporate HR, Administration, and Facility Management, but we are expanding into sectors like Medical and Healthcare, Pharma, Legal and Financial Services, Veterinary and Petcare, and Hospitality.

Our long-term goal is to build a strong community of business owners across various industries. We aim to support their business growth, expand their networks, and encourage effective collaboration through online and offline meetings and seminars. We are committed to providing our partners with the best opportunities to thrive, innovate, and lead in their respective fields.

Whether you're a guest, delegate, awardee, or partner, being associated with Procon Media offers numerous benefits, from networking opportunities with industry leaders to exploring potential business partnerships. We take pride in connecting you with quality vendors, helping you stay informed on industry trends, and offering you platforms for recognition and career advancement.
