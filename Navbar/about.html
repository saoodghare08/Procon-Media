About Us

Procon Media's vision for the future

At Procon Media, our vision is to create meaningful connections by bringing industry professionals together on a common platform through conferences, meetings, and exhibitions. Currently focused on Real Estate, Architecture, Corporate HR, Administration, and Facility Management, we plan to expand into sectors like Medical and Healthcare, Pharma, Legal and Financial Services, Veterinary and Petcare, and Hospitality.

Our long-term goal is to build a strong community of business owners across various industries. We aim to support their business growth, expand their networks, and encourage effective collaboration through online and offline meetings and seminars. We are committed to providing our partners with the best opportunities to thrive, innovate, and lead in their respective fields.

Procon Events & Entertainment

Procon Events and Entertainment, an offshoot of the Procon Group, is an end-to-end event management company dedicated to making your corporate events a seamless experience. We cater to a wide range of events, including conferences, meetings, employee engagement activities, dealer meets and RnR events.

Our specially dedicated team of professional event planners manages every aspect of your event, from ideation, planning, and execution to hotel booking, travel arrangements, event setup, and production. Whether it’s yoga sessions, fun football and cricket matches, self-defense workshops, canvas art workshops, Zumba sessions, or any other employee engagement activity, we've got it all covered.

With Procon Events and Entertainment, you can rest assured that your company’s events will be handled with the utmost care and professionalism.
