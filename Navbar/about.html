About Us

Procon Media's vision for the future

At Procon Media, our vision is to create meaningful connections by bringing industry professionals together on a common platform through conferences, meetings, and exhibitions. Currently focused on Real Estate, Architecture, Corporate HR, Administration, and Facility Management, we plan to expand into sectors like Medical and Healthcare, Pharma, Legal and Financial Services, Veterinary and Petcare, and Hospitality.

Our long-term goal is to build a strong community of business owners across various industries. We aim to support their business growth, expand their networks, and encourage effective collaboration through online and offline meetings and seminars. We are committed to providing our partners with the best opportunities to thrive, innovate, and lead in their respectiveÂ fields.

Benefits of Association with Procon Media

For Guests, Delegates, and Awardees:

Network with Industry Leaders: Connect with senior professionals from diverse sectors including Workplace, Administration, Facilities, Real Estate, and Architecture. Foster valuable relationships and collaborations.

Stay Informed on Industry Trends: Gain insights from various discussions and presentations. Keep updated on the latest industry developments and innovations.

Explore Career Opportunities: Engage with top leaders and companies. Potentially open doors to new job opportunities and career advancements.

Receive Prestigious Recognition: Participate in the Leadership Awards Ceremony. Be acknowledged for your contributions, offering a sense of accomplishment and public recognition.

Meet Quality Vendors: Network with reputable vendors during the conference. Explore potential partnerships and business opportunities.

Benefits of Association with Procon Media

for Conference Sponsors/Partners/Vendors:

Connect with Decision Makers: Network with corporate prospects and key decision-makers from various industries in a single day under one roof.

Showcase Your Offerings: Exhibit your products or services at a dedicated business stall in the exhibition area, allowing for one-on-one interactions with attendees.

Enhance Brand Presence: Position your brand as a leading and trusted entity within the industry, boosting your visibility and credibility.

Stage Opportunities: Gain the chance to speak on stage, representing your company to a direct audience of potential corporate clients and prospects.

Post-Event Networking Support: Receive assistance in connecting with guests and attendees, fostering relationships and opportunities post-conference as well.
