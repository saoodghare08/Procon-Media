Procon Events & Entertainments

Procon Events and Entertainment, an offshoot of the Procon Group, is an end-to-end event management company dedicated to making your corporate events a seamless experience. We cater to a wide range of events, including conferences, meetings, employee engagement activities, dealer meets, and RnR events.
Our specially dedicated team of professional event planners manages every aspect of your event, from ideation, planning, and execution to hotel booking, travel arrangements, event setup, and production.
Whether it’s yoga sessions, fun football and cricket matches, self-defense workshops, canvas art workshops, Zumba sessions, or any other employee engagement activity, we've got it all covered.
With Procon Events and Entertainment, you can rest assured that your company’s events will be handled with the utmost care and professionalism.
